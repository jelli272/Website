Dragon Ball FighterZ

Mechanics

Dragon Ball FigtherZ is a 2D fighting Game in much the same vein as Street Figther, but with a twist. It's a 3-on-3 fighting game where you are able to switch between any three characters on the fly during matches, thus ensuring you always keep your opponents on their toes. Mechanically, it's much the same as Street Figther in that the main goal is to get your opponents health down to zero by using combos, special attacks, and accurately reading your opponent.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to improve your gameplay:

  • Utilize the training mode extensively to practice combos, blockstrings, and assists for your team composition. You can train while waiting for matches which is a really useful feature.
  • Study high-level gameplay videos to understand advanced techniques, mix-ups, and strategies employed by experienced players.
  • Master the art of tagging in and out characters efficiently to maintain pressure, extend combos, and maximize your team's synergy during matches.
